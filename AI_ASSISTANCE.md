# AI Assistance Statement

*Versión en español: [`DECLARACION_ASISTENCIA_IA.md`](DECLARACION_ASISTENCIA_IA.md)*

This project was carried out with extensive artificial-intelligence assistance — **Claude and Claude Code (Anthropic)**, operating as directed agents. This statement exists because AI assistance **is part of the project's method, not a footnote**: what is documented here is, among other things, a case of empirical research directed by a non-programmer through AI agents, with the honesty controls (pre-registrations, look-ahead gates, placebos, adversarial audit) built precisely so that this technical delegation could not compromise the validity of the results.

## What the AI did (as a directed agent)

- The **technical implementation and execution** of all project code: GPU kernels, walk-forward pipeline, production bot, validation harness, experiments E01–E18 and their infrastructure.
- The **adversarial audit** of the verdicts themselves (multi-agent protocol, 39 agents ×2 passes; see [`auditoria_adversarial/`](auditoria_adversarial/README.md)).
- The **curation of this repository**: history sanitisation, structure, audit guides, and verification of every number against its primary artifact.
- **Assistance in drafting the manuscript** ([`paper/`](paper/)), generated from the table of verified claims ([`afirmaciones_respaldo.md`](afirmaciones_respaldo.md)) — never from memory or from summaries.

All of it **under the author's direction, review and approval**, task by task.

## What belongs to the author

- The **research questions** and the hypotheses (including the objectification of his own trading criterion, E05).
- The methodological and infrastructure **design decisions**.
- The **approval of every pre-registration** before its execution.
- The **sign-off of every verdict**.
- **Full responsibility for every published claim** in this repository and in the manuscript.

## Normative note

Under standard academic authorship criteria (**ICMJE, COPE**), AI tools do not qualify for authorship: they cannot take responsibility for the work nor approve its final version. The sole author of this work is **Ricardo Castellanos**, who assumes that responsibility in full.
